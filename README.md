## Why Choose LibreOffice?

- **Completely free and open source** ‚Äî MPL/LGPL license, no hidden fees  
- **Excellent compatibility** with Microsoft formats (.docx, .xlsx, .pptx, etc.)  
- **Native support** for the open standard **OpenDocument Format (ODF)**  
- **Regular updates** ‚Äî fresh versions released every 6‚Äì8 weeks  
- **Huge community** and thousands of extensions/templates  
- **Runs on Windows, macOS, Linux, and even in the browser** (LibreOffice Online)

Your documents will look **professional** and polished ‚Äî whether it's a letter, thesis, brochure, report, presentation, or technical drawing.
